=== Black — how to play ===

1. Make sure all the files are in THIS folder:
   - black.exe
   - libraylib.dll
   - glfw3.dll
   - desk_server.txt
   - desk_auth.txt
   - desk_voice.txt

2. Double-click black.exe.

3. First time: click "New here? Create an account", pick a username + password
   (6+ characters), then Create account.

4. Click "Enter Lobby", then "Play 1v1" to join the match.

Controls in the 1v1:
   WASD = move,  Mouse = look,  Left click = shoot,  Esc = back to lobby

Notes:
- The host (whoever shares this) must have their PC + servers + ngrok running,
  or you'll be stuck on "connecting...". That's normal if their PC is off.
- Movement may look a little steppy over the internet. That's expected.
- Voice chat only works on the same local network, not over the internet.
